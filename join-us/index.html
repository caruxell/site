[DEV] Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)

If you are excited to solve complex problems of software reliability and scalability, implement complex systems, then you might be the perfect candidate for this job!
Here's what we are looking for in our ideal candidate:
  • Strong understanding of complex data structures and algorithms
  • Very good communication and writing skills (english and romanian)
  • At least 5 years of experience in scaling systems, database management, load balancing
  • At least 5 years of experience with at least one of the following: NodeJS, Golang, C#, C++
  • At least 5 years of experience with game engines like UnrealEngine / Unity
  • Deep knowledge and understanding of security principles
  • Ability to work in a team of likeminded people, and contribute with solutions and ideas to solve complex technical problems
  • At least 5 years of experience in animations and web based graphical interaction (webGL, SVG animation, WebAssembly, Emscripten)
  • experience with Unix/Linux operating systems
Our preferred candidate will also have:
  • Proven experience with the above mentioned technologies (portfolio)
  • Some basic experience in working with 3D modeling software & animations (Blender, Cinema4D)
  • Knowledge of game development is highly appreciated, no matter of the game engine
  • has used WebAssembly, Emscripten, WebVR, WebAR, WebGL in his projects
  • has used data visualisation frameworks/libraries and animation libraries like three.js, animejs, greensock
  • Bachelor's degree, Master's degree, or PhD in Computer Science or a related technical field
You will get:
  • stock options
  • competitive salary
  • industry leading private health insurance
  • gym membership pay off
  • free coffee & fruits on site
